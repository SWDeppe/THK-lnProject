Iron Will

Iron Will is a combat-focused action RPG with deep customization. Players use a modular mechanical arm, choosing different weapons and tools to adapt their fighting style. The game features a semi-open world with five cities, each offering unique challenges, enemies, and hidden dungeons. Exploration and resource gathering allow players to upgrade their abilities and uncover the world’s secrets. Designed for PC and consoles, Iron Will combines fast-paced combat, RPG progression, and environmental storytelling. Its stylized 1900s-inspired world blends industrial-era aesthetics with mechanical advancements, appealing to players who enjoy both strategic gameplay and immersive world-building.
